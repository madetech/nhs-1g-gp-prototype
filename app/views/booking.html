Book online if you can

If possible we prefer you to request an appointment online. This helps us prioritise patients who need the most help more efficiently. You'll need to have registered your email address or mobile number with us before you can use this service. Register now.

Online booking follows a 3 step process.

  1. Step 1 Request your appointment online

    You'll need to provide some basic information about your medical issue such as symptoms you are experiencing and how long you have had them.

  2. Step 2 Initial triage

    A Care Navigator at the practice will review your request and assign you the most appropriate type of appointment. This could be a face-to-face consultation with a GP, a phone consultation or a consultation with a nurse or Advanced Nurse Practitioner. Occasioanlly they may refer you to a different resource or external service if this is more appropriate.

  3. Step 3 Choose your appointment time

    We will send you a link via email or text message. This will allow you to view the appointment dates and times. If appropriate you will be able to choose whether you prefer a phone call or to come in to see us face-to-face. at this stage we may also ask you some additional questions or ask you to upload a photo if that is appropriate.
